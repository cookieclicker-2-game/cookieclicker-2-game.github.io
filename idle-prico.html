Introduction to Idle Pricon

Idle Pricon is an idle management game where you operate a prison facility and grow it into a complex correctional system. Upgrade guards, improve cells, expand security areas, and automate workflows as your prison grows larger and more profitable.

How to Play Idle Pricon

Start with a small detention block and a few guards. Earn income from prisoner processing and daily operations, then reinvest it into better equipment, new units, faster workflows, and expanded areas. Over time, your prison evolves from basic holding cells into a fully automated mega-facility.

Tips and Strategies

  • Upgrade guard movement speed to reduce bottlenecks.
  • Expand your processing areas early to gain more income sources.
  • Balance upgrades between cells, guards, and workflow automation.
