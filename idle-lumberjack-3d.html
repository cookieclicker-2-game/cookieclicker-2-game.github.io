Introduction to Idle Lumberjack 3D

Idle Lumberjack 3D is a chill idle game where you play as a hard-working lumberjack in a 3D forest. Chop down trees, collect logs, sell them for coins, and use those coins to upgrade your tools and unlock new forest areas.

How to Play Idle Lumberjack 3D

Move your lumberjack through the forest and cut trees to gather wood. Once your backpack is full, walk back to the selling point to convert logs into coins. Spend coins on better axes, increased capacity, or new zones with more valuable trees so your income grows over time.

Tips and Strategies

  • Upgrade your axe first to cut trees faster and reduce grind.
  • Increase backpack capacity so you make fewer trips back to the shop.
  • Unlock new forest zones as soon as you can for better rewards per tree.
