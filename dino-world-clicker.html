Introduction to Dino World: Clicker

Dino World: Clicker is a prehistoric themed clicker game where every tap grows your dinosaur settlement. Collect resources, unlock new dinos, and evolve your tiny camp into a thriving Jurassic kingdom.

How to Play Dino World: Clicker

Click on the dinosaurs and objects to gain resources. Spend them on upgrades that increase production, unlock new species, and automate parts of your world. The more you invest, the faster your numbers rise.

Tips and Strategies

  • Upgrade your best-producing dinosaurs first for maximum efficiency.
  • Balance between active clicking power and idle income upgrades.
  • Check in often to spend saved resources on major evolution tiers.
