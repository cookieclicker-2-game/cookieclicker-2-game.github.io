Introduction to BloodMoney

BloodMoney is a dark-themed idle clicker where every tap adds to your underground fortune. Start small, make risky moves, and slowly turn your side hustle into a powerful money machine.

How to Play BloodMoney

Click to earn your first stacks of cash, then invest in upgrades that automate the income. The deeper you go, the faster your profits grow. Your goal is simple: keep the money counter climbing and unlock the most profitable upgrades possible.

Tips and Strategies

  • Prioritize upgrades that boost passive income so your cash grows even while idle.
  • Use burst clicking after major upgrades to quickly recover your investment.
  • Keep an eye on cost scaling and avoid overbuying low-value upgrades.
