Introduction to Sprunki Clicker Master

Sprunki Clicker Master is a goofy, chaotic, meme-fueled clicker game inspired by the Sprunki universe. Tap frantically, unlock bizarre characters, and enjoy a silly idle experience filled with strange animations and upgrades.

How to Play Sprunki Clicker Master

Click rapidly to earn points, unlock new Sprunki-style characters, and invest in upgrades that multiply your clicking power. Let the game idle to accumulate earnings even when you're not tapping.

Tips and Strategies

  • Unlock new characters early for better multipliers.
  • Invest in idle upgrades for long-term gains.
  • Use burst clicking during special events for big rewards.
