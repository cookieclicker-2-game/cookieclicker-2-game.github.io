Introduction to Police Evolution Idle

Police Evolution Idle is an idle management game where you grow a full police department from the ground up. Recruit rookie officers, upgrade training facilities, deploy patrol units, and evolve your HQ into a powerful law-enforcement organization.

How to Play Police Evolution Idle

Start with a small group of officers and basic tasks. As criminals are caught and missions are completed, you'll earn money to expand your HQ. Upgrade departments, unlock detectives, SWAT teams, vehicles, and higher-tier officers to keep your city safe.

Tips and Strategies

  • Upgrade training areas early to speed up officer evolution.
  • Balance patrol units and HQ upgrades to prevent bottlenecks.
  • Unlock high-tier units as soon as possible for large income boosts.
