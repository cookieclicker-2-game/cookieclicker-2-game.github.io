Introduction to Dancing Beat

Dancing Beat is a colorful rhythm game where you tap, hold, and slide to the beat of energetic music tracks. Hit notes at the right time to keep your combo going and chase high scores.

How to Play Dancing Beat

Use your mouse, keyboard, or touch controls to tap on the notes as they reach the hit area. The more accurately you time your taps, the higher your score and combo multiplier will be. Missing notes breaks your streak and lowers your final rating.
