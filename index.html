Introduction to Cookie Clicker 2

Cookie Clicker 2 is a free online clicker game where every tap on the giant cookie helps you grow a crazy, ever-expanding bakery empire. Buy auto-clickers, hire grandmas, unlock factories, and watch your cookies-per-second skyrocket.

How to Play Cookie Clicker 2

Click the big cookie to earn your first batch of cookies, then invest them into buildings and upgrades that generate cookies automatically. The deeper you go, the more powerful and absurd your production chain becomes.

Tips and Strategies

  • Focus early on cheap upgrades that boost your manual clicks.
  • Balance between buying new buildings and upgrading existing ones.
  • Check in often to spend your offline cookie production efficiently.
