Introduction to Curve Rush IO

Curve Rush IO is a fast-paced multiplayer io game where you leave a glowing trail behind you as you dash around the arena. Your goal is simple: avoid crashing into any trails while forcing other players to slam into yours.

How to Play Curve Rush IO

Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to steer your character and draw smooth or sharp curves. Try to cut off other players by crossing their path, making them collide with your trail. The longer you survive, the higher your score climbs on the leaderboard.

Tips and Strategies
