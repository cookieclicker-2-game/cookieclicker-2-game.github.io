Introduction to Idle Idol

Idle Idol is an idle management game where you guide a rising star on their journey to fame. Train your idol, perform shows, and unlock upgrades that increase your popularity and income over time.

How to Play Idle Idol

Start by sending your idol to basic performances and training sessions. Each activity earns fans and coins, which you can reinvest into better training, marketing, and new venues. As you upgrade, your idol performs more efficiently and earns a lot more even while you are away.

Tips and Strategies

  • Focus first on upgrades that increase passive earnings from performances.
  • Invest in training to unlock higher-tier shows with better payouts.
  • Check back regularly to reinvest your income and keep progression smooth.
